Food of Iloilo - La Paz Batchoy

Batchoy, alternatively spelled batsoy, is a Filipino noodle soup of pork offal, crushed pork cracklings, chicken stock, beef

loin, and round noodles. The original and most popular variant, the La Paz batchoy, traces its roots to the Iloilo City

district of La Paz.
