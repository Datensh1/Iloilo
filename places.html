Megaworld

Megaworld Iloilo is a large-scale real estate development project by the Megaworld Corporation in Iloilo City. It includes a mix of residential, commercial, and business spaces designed to create a modern and vibrant urban environment. This development aims to contribute to the growth and economic development of Iloilo City.

Jaro Cathedral

The Jaro Cathedral, also known as the National Shrine of Our Lady of Candles, is a Roman Catholic cathedral located in Jaro, Iloilo City. It is one of the oldest and most important religious sites in the Philippines. The cathedral is known for its distinct architecture and is a significant pilgrimage site, especially during the Feast of Our Lady of Candles.

Esplanade

The Iloilo Esplanade is a scenic riverside promenade along the Iloilo River in Iloilo City. It is a popular destination for both locals and tourists, offering a relaxing environment for leisurely walks, jogging, and enjoying the beautiful river views. The esplanade is also lined with restaurants and shops, making it a vibrant part of the city's recreational and dining scene.

Museo Iloilo

Museo Iloilo is a museum in Iloilo City that showcases the cultural heritage and history of the region. It features a wide range of artifacts, artworks, and exhibits that provide insights into the rich cultural and historical heritage of Iloilo and its people.

Fort San Pedro

Fort San Pedro in Iloilo is a historic Spanish-era fortress located in the city of Iloilo, Philippines. It was originally built in 1616 for defensive purposes during the Spanish colonial period. The fort has a triangular shape and was used to protect the area from pirate attacks. Today, it serves as a museum and cultural heritage site, offering visitors a glimpse into the region's history.

Molo Church

The Molo Church, officially known as the St. Anne Parish Church, is a historic church located in Molo, Iloilo City. It is often referred to as the "Women's Church" due to the prominent role of women in its history. The church is known for its Gothic-inspired architecture and is a significant religious and cultural landmark in Iloilo.
