ABOUT US

Iloilo City is a highly urbanized city in the Philippines, located on the island of Panay in the Visayas region. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, historic sites, and vibrant festivals. Iloilo City is often called the "City of Love" due to its friendly and welcoming atmosphere

The city is known for its vibrant culture and festivals. Dinagyang Festival, celebrated in January, is one of the most famous festivals in the Philippines, featuring colorful parades and traditional dances. The Iloilo Paraw Regatta is a traditional sailboat race held in Iloilo City, Philippines. It usually takes place every February and is a significant cultural event in the region. The race features colorful paraw sailboats, which are traditional outrigger boats.

Notable landmarks include the historic Molo Church, Jaro Cathedral, and the Iloilo Business Park, a modern commercial and residential development.

Iloilo is famous for its delicious cuisine, La Paz Batchoy.

Iloilo City is a blend of history, culture, and economic development, making it an important destination in the Philippines.
